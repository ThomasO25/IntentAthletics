Format One-on-one, in-person sessions

Youth Training

Semi-Private Training

Train alongside two or three people. Everyone in the session has their own individualized program — this is not a group class. You share the time and the space, but your program is built for you.

A good fit for friends or training partners who want to work with John, or for anyone who wants individualized programming at a more accessible price than fully private sessions.

Format Small group — 2 to 3 people per session
Who it's for Friends, training partners, or anyone wanting a personalized program at a shared price
Includes Individual program for each person · In-session coaching · Progress tracking · Nutritional guidance
Important Each person trains their own program — not a shared group workout
